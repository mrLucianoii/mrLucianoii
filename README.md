# Luciano

### Founder, **Octonions** — a private AI agent, and the guild that runs behind it. 🎯 Pre-launch, heads-down.

I build multi-agent systems that can actually *do* things — plan, delegate to one another, and act
on your behalf, with a human kept in the loop wherever an action could do damage.

Solo-built over ~2.5 years: dozens of services on Kubernetes, a Flutter app on macOS & iOS, a
crew of coordinated agents, RAG on pgvector, and a human-approval safety layer on every
destructive action.

**The thing I'm proudest of isn't a feature — it's a referee.**
I built tooling that reads my entire codebase and reports its structure back as ground truth, then
tracks how much of its own account is *guessed* versus *verified* — and holds the guessing under
1%. It exists to stop me from quietly upgrading "planned" to "shipped." By that same measurement,
the two most central abstractions in the whole system are **identity** and **permission**.
Security-first by measurement, not by slogan.
